Faculty of Chemical Engineering and Biotechnologies

Introduction

This faculty trains engineers in various specializations in the chemical industry. The faculty has set up new specializations, laboratories and research centers to meet the demand for technological and scientific development.

The Departments in this Faculty

  • Organic Chemistry
  • Inorganic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry and Electrochemistry Department
  • Chemical and Biochemical Engineering Department
  • Analytical Chemistry and Environmental Engineering Department
  • Bioresources and Polymer Science Department
  • General Chemistry Department
  • Science and Engineering of Oxide Materials and Nanomaterials Department

The National Conference of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering

Every two years, The Faculty of Chemical Engineering and Biotechnologies coordinates the National Conference of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, taking an active part in the national and overseas scientific publications.
