The Faculty of Aerospace Engineering

Introduction

The faculty teaches students and provides them with the necessary skills relating to the field of aerospace engineering and other related fields.The engineers trained in the faculty are educated to be open minded, able to adapt themselves to any new technical and scientific requirements, as well as to problems related to engineering management, wherever they work.

The Departments in this Faculty

  • Aerospace Sciences "Elie Carafoli" Department
  • Aeronautical Systems Engineering "Nicolae Tipei" Department
  • Engineering Graphics and Industrial Design Department
