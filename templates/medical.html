Medical Engineering

Introduction

The Faculty of Medical Engineering provides graduates with theoretical and practical skills in the field of medical engineering, by forming skills for technical and informatics assistance, for engineering research, innovation and design - in the field of medical equipment, devices and materials with properties specific to medical applications, for creating software dedicated to applications in the medical field.

The Departments in this Faculty

  • Bioengineering and Biotechnology Department
  • Department of Physical Education and Sport - Physical Therapy

Partnerships and Collaborations

In partnership with the 'Carol Davila' Medical University of Bucharest, the necessary medical training is offered to students. Multiple collaborations including ones with companies of medical equipment and devices, with national institutes of research and development of the clinical environment, and more, provide chances for the best insertion of graduates into the national and international professional environment.
